Lead-to-Cash

This document outlines the end-to-end Lead-to-Cash process, which covers the entire customer journey from initial engagement to final revenue collection.

1. Process Definition

The Lead-to-Cash (L2C) process is a cross-functional, end-to-end business process that encompasses all the steps required to identify potential customers (leads), engage them to win their business, deliver the agreed-upon training services, and manage the resulting revenue and payment collection.

2. Objectives

The primary objectives of the L2C process are to:

  • Provide a seamless and positive experience for our clients throughout their entire journey with Valor Egypt.
  • Maximize revenue generation by efficiently converting leads into paying customers.
  • Ensure operational efficiency by clarifying roles, responsibilities, and hand-offs between departments.
  • Improve cash flow by ensuring timely and accurate billing and collections.
  • Provide clear visibility into the sales pipeline, service delivery status, and revenue forecast.

3. Scope

The process begins when a potential customer is identified (a new lead) and ends when the final payment for the delivered services has been received and reconciled in our financial system. It covers all marketing, sales, service delivery, and financial activities related to a customer engagement.

5. Stakeholders

This end-to-end process involves collaboration across multiple functions. Key stakeholders include:

  • Clients: The external participants who initiate the process and receive our services.
  • Sales & Marketing Team: Responsible for lead generation, nurturing, proposals, and closing deals.
  • Training & Consulting Team: The subject matter experts who define and deliver the services.
  • Finance & Admin Team: Responsible for contracts, invoicing, collections, and financial reporting.

6. Processes Included

The L2C flow is comprised of several Level 3 processes from our SOP Framework. The primary components are:

3.5.1 Manage leads/opportunities

Systematically track and nurture potential client inquiries and opportunities from initial contact through to the proposal stage.

3.5.3 Develop and manage sales proposals

Create and deliver compelling, customized training proposals and quotes that clearly address client requirements and objectives.

3.5.4 Manage sales orders

Process confirmed training engagements, ensuring all contractual details are accurately recorded and communicated to the delivery team.

5.3.1 Initiate service delivery

Formally kick-off training engagements by confirming scope, aligning with client objectives, and mobilizing the necessary training resources.

5.3.2 Execute service delivery

Conduct the training sessions as planned, managing all logistical and instructional aspects to ensure a high-quality learning experience.

9.2.2 Invoice customer

Generate and transmit accurate invoices to clients following the completion of training services, as per contractual terms.

9.2.3 Process accounts receivable (AR)

Manage incoming client payments, apply them to outstanding invoices, and follow up on any delinquent accounts.
