Design-to-Publish

This document outlines the end-to-end Design-to-Publish process, which covers the entire journey from course creation to final publication and marketing.

1. Process Definition

The Design-to-Publish (D2P) process is a comprehensive framework that guides the creation, development, and launch of training courses. It encompasses everything from initial course design and content creation to marketing, pricing strategy, and final publication.

2. Objectives

The primary objectives of the D2P process are to:

  • Ensure consistent quality in course design and content development
  • Streamline the course creation and publication process
  • Maximize course visibility and marketability
  • Maintain effective communication with all stakeholders
  • Establish clear pricing strategies aligned with market demands

3. Scope

The process begins with the initial course concept and design phase and concludes with the successful publication and marketing of the course. It includes all aspects of course development, session planning, speaker management, pricing strategy, marketing material creation, and communication planning.

5. Stakeholders

This end-to-end process involves collaboration across multiple functions. Key stakeholders include:

  • Course Design Team: Responsible for curriculum development and content creation
  • Training Coordinators: Handle session planning and speaker management
  • Marketing Team: Develops and executes marketing strategies and materials
  • Finance Team: Manages pricing strategies and payment systems
  • External Speakers: Subject matter experts who deliver the training

6. Processes Included

The D2P flow encompasses several Level 3 processes from our SOP Framework. The primary components are:

4.1.1 Course Design & Development

Create and structure course content, including learning objectives, curriculum, and materials.

4.1.2 Session Planning

Schedule and organize training sessions, including logistics and resource allocation.

4.2.1 Speaker Management

Identify, vet, and coordinate with subject matter experts and trainers.

6.3.1 Pricing Strategy

Develop and implement pricing models aligned with market conditions and business goals.

7.1.1 Marketing Materials

Create and manage promotional content and marketing collateral.

7.1.2 Digital Presence

Develop and maintain landing pages and online course presence.

7.2.1 Communication Planning

Plan and execute communication strategies for course promotion and engagement.
